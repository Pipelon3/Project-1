Our goal

Our goal is to create a universal entertainment database that will search multiple APIs to find the perfect result!

How to use

Just simply think of your favorite Movie, Show, or Game and click on its tab and type the name in the search bar. After hitting submit we will browse the web for your search and pull up useful information about the query!

How it works

Our advance search uses a variety of different APIs to search multiple databases to find the exact information and display it in a convenient way that is easy to read.
